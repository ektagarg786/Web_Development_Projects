About Us

Welcome to Ali Baba Restaurant . We have been providing fresh , fast , and friendly food . Ali Baba isn’t just about authentic food, it specializes in high standard, fresh and healthy food with affordable prices. Our food is cooked fresh to the customer’s order and taste. You are guaranteed the freshness of your order. At the head of the team are the restaurant’s four owners: Vikram Sarkar , Tamanna Sain , Naina Kashyap and Ekta. The group shares a true love for the flavourful cooking.

The culinary experience at Ali Baba is enhanced by a courteous service and an appealing decor. The menu’s specialties, as combined with a pleasant atmosphere, are sure to satisfy your longing for delectable food enjoyed in the good company of friends.

Our Mission

Increase customer satisfaction and loyalty by providing the fastest and most accurate service , fresh products in the friendliest and cleanest environment.we accomplish this through teamwork , communication , honesty , fun and sharing experiences as we all grow together.

Community Outreach

Donations

We are givers and we try to fulfill as many donation requests as possible. Pizza My Heart is especially interested in helping schools and non-profits within our delivery zones. Annually, we end up donating thousands of gift certificates and free slice cards. If you’ve got a good cause, please e-mail Donations@Maemo.com and tell us about your event, who it benefits, and why we should help out. We prefer email because it’s fast, and eco-friendly. Remember to include your contact information and mailing address in the email.

Scrip Program

We believe there is no better investment in our community than supporting our local schools. Students are our future leaders, customers and friends. With that in mind, for use in school fundraising programs we sell our:
$5 gift cards for $4.40
$10 gift cards for $8.50
$20 gift cards for $16.00
Is your school signed up with us yet? Please e-mail Allison@pizzamyheart.com to get started in the program.

Fundraisers

Looking to raise money for your charitable organization? Holding a fundraiser at your local Pizza My Heart is not only fun and easy, but as long as your event sales are $100 or more, a whopping 30% donation goes back to your group from the sales during your event!
